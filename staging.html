Staging

Looks do say it all

Properties look better and sell for higher prices when they have that "home" feel and prospective buyers can visualise how big a room is and what can be done with the space.

We can offer a house full of furniture or simply advice on positioning of what you already have. We can help you improve that all important first impression – selecting the right colours, deciding what to display and how to position the furniture, as well as what things to hide away. Our maintenance team can tidy the front garden and mow the lawns on a regular basis, espacially when the property is empty and on the market.

Full House Staging

Our home stylist will visist and liaise with you about how to get the best from our service and plan for your marketing campaign. Our careful team will deliver furniture and ornaments for up to a 4 bedroom home, set them in place and leave the property ready for the first open home. Once the property has sold we will come and remove everything, leaving the house ready for the new owners.

Partial Staging or Working with what you have

Our home stylist will visit and liaise with you about how to get the best of our service and plan for your marketing campaign. They will advise on what to hide in the garage or cupboards, positioning of beds in a room and that all important first impression.
