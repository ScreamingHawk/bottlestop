As owners of investment properties ourselves we know what is important to create and manage a successful portfolio. We believe in providing better homes for people and this shows in everything we do.

Case Study: Kitchen Project

This kitchen was small, not very user friendly and separated from the living. We wanted to open the kitchen and improve the flow, as the living already had a ranch slider opening to outside. The cabinetry and appliances where also outdated and the position of the fridge blocked the entrance to the kitchen when the door was open.

The flow was improved by removing a partial wall, but retaining the pantry next to it. This allowed for a waist high cabinet to be put in its place, maintaining the storage space and creating a work area for conversation and also a serving bench to guests in the lounge. A specific fridge area was moved to the other wall, also with new cabinets, bench top and oven / range hood. A pressed tin splash back was added to give a unique feature and style as well as new vinyl flooring to complete the space.
